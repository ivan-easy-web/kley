Natalina Jewellery is a Finnish jewellery brand, launched in 2019 by two talented friends - Natasha and Lina. The brand Natalina creates bright, joyous, genuine jewellery for strong and modern women. Behind each collection and name, there is a story about the gemstones used and their particular myths and history.

Our work was to build a feminine, elegant, bright and joyful brand. During Spring and Summer we have created a brand identity, logo, fonts, color scheme, printed materials, business cards, packagings, flyers, gift card samples and executed a web shop building.

Since that time Natalina jewellery has launched multiple collections and been granted “Design from Finland” mark, and we are still working with them to help shape digitally new collections.
