Haru Sushi project is one of our long lasting clients with whom we have worked since almost the beginning. We came when the second restaurant just opened and there was no social media at all. Our main goal was at first to introduce people to Haru and grow interest. During the years Haru sushi became not only the favorite sushi places for all young people around the center but one of the biggest sushi brands in Helsinki.

Haru Sushi is known for having extremely high quality of all ingredients, traditional taste of Japanese sushi and at the same time modern approach and design of the restaurants. We are trying to highlight that and combine the culture of Japanese cuisine with minimalistic design and colors.

The account style is very easy going, targeted at young audience. It includes a lot of communication with the audience, stories and even sometimes memes:) During the first year we were actively engaging bloggers and influencers to grow the audience, and now one of the big aims is to increase UGC (User-generated Content) and ER (Engagement Rate).
